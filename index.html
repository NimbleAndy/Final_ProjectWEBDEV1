Our Story

Uplift Fitness emerged from a shared passion for personal transformation and well-being. The name reflects our commitment to uplifting lives through fitness. We believe in fostering a positive community where individuals are inspired to surpass their limits and achieve holistic well-being. Uplift Fitness is not just a place to work out; it's a destination for positive change, growth, and a healthier, happier lifestyle. Join us in this empowering journey towards a better you.

No more struggling to make rigid gym classes work with your schedule. Our personalized training means you can work out on your own time, in your own space, and design customized programs that fit your exact needs and abilities. Want to exercise first thing in the morning? Only have access to minimal equipment? Have a packed work week with unpredictable hours? Trisha

Her vibrant energy and passion for fitness create an atmosphere where every member feels motivated and empowered. Ava

Flexibility Training, Physical Therapy

Ava is not just a fitness instructor; she is a former dancer and ballerina with a passion for enhancing flexibility and promoting overall well-being. Join her to discover a new level of flexibility and strength while enjoying the artistry of movement.
